About InAmigos Foundation

Established in September 2020, InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered nonprofit headquartered in Chhattisgarh, India. We operate on the principle of collective action to address social challenges across the nation.

Certifications & Recognition:

  • ✓ 80G Registration
  • ✓ 12A Recognition
  • ✓ ISO 9001:2015 Certified

We work in partnership with government and corporate sectors to create lasting, positive change in the lives of vulnerable populations across India.

Our Projects & Initiatives

Six transformative programs addressing critical social needs

🍽️

Sewa

Food & Clothing Assistance

Providing essential food and clothing to economically disadvantaged groups, ensuring basic dignity and survival needs are met.

📚

Bachpanshala

Educational Access

Bringing quality education to underprivileged children, breaking the cycle of poverty through learning and skill development.

🐾

Jeev

Animal Welfare

Rescuing and protecting animals through compassionate care, rehabilitation, and community awareness about animal rights.

👩‍💼

Udaan

🌱

Prakriti

Environmental Conservation

Protecting our environment through sustainability initiatives, awareness campaigns, and community-based conservation efforts.

💼

Vikas

Employment Readiness

Preparing individuals for the job market through comprehensive training and placement support services.
